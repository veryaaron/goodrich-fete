Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The Goodrich Summer Fete website is run by volunteers for the benefit of our local community. We take your privacy seriously and keep things simple.

What we collect

When you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your area of interest (e.g. volunteering, running a stall)
  • Any message you choose to include

We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising. We do not collect any data beyond what you voluntarily submit through the contact form.

How we use it

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Coordinate volunteering or stall bookings for the fete

That's it. We will never sell, share, or use your data for marketing or promotional purposes.

How we store it

Your submissions are stored securely using Cloudflare's infrastructure. We keep submissions for up to one year to help us plan the fete, after which they are automatically deleted.

Third parties

We use Resend to deliver email notifications of form submissions to our team. No other third parties have access to your data. We do not share your information with sponsors, partners, or anyone else.

Your rights

You can ask us to:

  • Tell you what data we hold about you
  • Delete your data
  • Correct any inaccurate information

Just drop us an email at hello@goodrichfete.com and we'll sort it out promptly.

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll note the date at the top of this page. Nothing complicated.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email us at hello@goodrichfete.com.
