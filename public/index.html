Welcome

A Celebration of
Community & Countryside

The Goodrich Summer Fete is a beloved tradition bringing together our village and visitors for a glorious day of fun, food, and friendly competition in the beautiful grounds of The Old Vicarage.

From classic fete games and spirited competitions to mouthwatering food stalls and live music, there's something for absolutely everyone. Whether you're a seasoned competitor in the Great Goodrich Bake-Off or just fancy a go at the coconut shy, we'd love to see you there.

All proceeds go directly back into our local community.

What's On

Fun for Everyone

A day packed with traditional entertainment, games, and activities for all ages.

Coconut Shy

Test your aim at the classic fairground favourite. Three balls, one prize — can you knock one down?

Splat the Rat

Quick reflexes needed! Watch the pipe and splat the rat as it drops. Harder than it looks.

Bouncy Castles

Hours of bouncing fun for the little ones. Multiple inflatables to keep them entertained all afternoon.

Fire Service Demo

Our local fire service puts on a thrilling demonstration. A highlight for the kids (and big kids too).

Live Music

Enjoy the Roaring Megs choir, bandeoke singalongs, and more live performances throughout the day.

And Much More

Tombola, raffle, plant stalls, craft stands, and plenty of surprises on the day. Something for everyone.

Competitions

Show Off Your Talents

Four fantastic competitions with categories for all ages. Entry is free — bring your best!

01

The Great Goodrich Bake-Off

Our legendary baking competition returns with categories for Victoria Sponge, Coffee & Walnut, Lemon Drizzle, Brownies, Decorated Cakes, and Children's Cupcakes (under 12s).

Entries by 09:45 at The Old Vicarage on fete day
02

Growers Competition

Show off your green fingers with tray displays and flower arrangements. All produce must be self-grown.

Entries from 08:30 on fete day
03

Dog Show

Best Trick, Most Like Owner, Fancy Dress, Veteran, Prettiest Bitch, Most Handsome, Waggiest Tail, and Temptation Alley.

Registration at 3pm at the fete
04

Children's Art Competition

This year's theme: The River Wye. 2D entries only, A3 maximum. Age groups: Under 7, 8–11, and Over 12.

Entries by 09:00 on fete day
